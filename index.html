About Us

Expertly crafted metalwork for functional elegance and security

At Vazquez Metals, we specialize in crafting custom metal doors, windows, gates, railings, and other metalwork. Our skilled craftsmen use only the finest materials and techniques to produce products that are not only functional but also visually stunning. We work closely with our clients to ensure that every piece we create meets their unique needs and enhances the overall beauty and security of their space. Contact us today to discuss your project and discover how we can bring your vision to life with our exceptional metalwork.

Manufacturing

Our state-of-the-art facilities and advanced equipment enable us to produce top-quality metal products with precision and efficiency. From custom doors and windows to intricate metal designs, we ensure that every piece is expertly crafted to meet your specifications and exceed your expectations.

Fabrication

Our skilled craftsmen are masters of metal fabrication. With meticulous attention to detail, we transform raw materials into works of art. Whether it's welding, cutting, bending, or shaping, we employ industry-leading techniques to create durable and visually stunning metalwork. Count on us for flawless fabrication that brings your designs to life.

Installation

With careful planning and precise execution, we bring your metal creations to your space, ensuring they fit perfectly and are securely installed.
